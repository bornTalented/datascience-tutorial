Project Name: Funnel Optimization – Lead to Appointment & Appointment to Login
Staff Role: Data Scientist

Responsibilities
- Designed and developed two key sub-funnels—“Lead to Appointment” and “Appointment to Login”—as part of the ETLI funnel optimization project for a financial services client.
- Owned the entire ML workflow including feature engineering, model development, training, and validation.
- Applied advanced preprocessing techniques and handled missing data, outliers, and categorical encoding to improve model robustness.
- Evaluated multiple algorithms (XGBoost, Logistic Regression, Random Forest) using performance metrics like AUC, Precision, and Recall.
- Worked closely with business teams to map funnel definitions and ensured model outputs translated into actionable insights for improving lead conversion.
