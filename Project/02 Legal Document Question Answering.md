Project Name: Legal Document Question Answering
Staff Role: Data Scientist

Responsibilities
- Developed a hybrid NLP system for information retrieval from lengthy legal documents, designed to extract answers from unstructured text based on a predefined list of questions.
- Combined rule-based heuristics with transformer-based language models to maximize answer accuracy in complex, domain-specific contexts.
- Implemented similarity-based reranking techniques to improve relevancy of retrieved answers.
